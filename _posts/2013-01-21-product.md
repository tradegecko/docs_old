####   Update a product

######     Request:
Method     | Request URL   
-----------| ------------- 
**PUT**    | *https://api.tradegecko.com/products/{PRODUCT_ID}*

###### Return:
      Returns 204 status when the product gets updated successfully.

######     Request:
Method     | Request URL
